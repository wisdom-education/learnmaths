BASIC ARITHMETIC OPERATIONS

1.Addition

Positive No + Positive No = Positive No , eg: 8 + 2 = 10

Negative No + Negative No = Negative No , eg: -5 + -2 = -7

Negative No + Positive No = Difference of No's and sign of greater No , eg: -7 + 5 = -2

2.Multiplication

Positive No * Positive No = Positive No , eg: 3 * 2 = 6

Negative No * Negative No = Positive No , eg: -4 * -2 = 8

Positive No * Negative No = Negative No , eg: 5 * -2 = -10

3.Division

Positive No * Positive No = Positive No , eg: 3 * 2 = 6

Negative No * Negative No = Positive No , eg: -4 * -2 = 8

Positive No * Negative No = Negative No , eg: 5 * -2 = -10
